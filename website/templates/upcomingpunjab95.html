Storyline

Based on the challenging life of Jaswant Singh Khalra, a tenacious and brave Sikh rights activist who gained international recognition for his ground-breaking research including 25,000 unlawful killings, countless disappearances, and secretive cremations carried out by the Punjab Police. This narrative highlights his determination to bring about change prior to his kidnapping by the Punjab Police, torture, and brutal murder.
